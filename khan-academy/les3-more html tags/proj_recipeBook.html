Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup

Other ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk and egg together, then combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Heat a skillet and pour batter to form pancakes. Cook until golden.

Recipe source: AllRecipes

Cheese Omelette

Ingredient Amount
Eggs 2
Cheese 50 g

Other ingredients:

  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp butter

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour eggs into pan, cook slightly, then add cheese.
  4. Fold omelette and serve hot.

Recipe source: Simply Recipes

Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredient Amount
Lettuce 2 cups
Tomatoes 1 cup

Other ingredients:

  • Cucumber slices
  • Olives
  • Olive oil and lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix in a large bowl and drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Toss gently and serve fresh.

Recipe source: AllRecipes
