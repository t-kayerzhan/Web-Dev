HTML Headings

Headings are titles on a web page. They show the structure of the content.

There are 6 heading levels: h1 (most important) to h6 (least important).

Heading 1

Main page title (usually one).

Heading 2

Section title.

Heading 3

Subsection title.

Heading 4

Less important.

Heading 5

Minor heading.

Heading 6

Least important.

Heading 1

Style changes size, not importance.
